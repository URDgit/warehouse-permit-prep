## How Ontario handles HPS permits

- Record prefix is **FC-** (Fire module) — unlike Fontana there is **no
  HPS-specific record-type filter**, so the sweep method is **month-window
  queries** over the FC series and manual HPS identification.
- **Volume: ~50–75 HPS filings/year — roughly 2–3× Fontana.** Ontario is the
  bigger pond.
